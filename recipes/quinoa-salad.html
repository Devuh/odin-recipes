Quinoa Salad

A refreshing and nutritious quinoa salad packed with vibrant flavors!

This colorful quinoa salad is a perfect balance of textures and flavors. Bursting with fresh vegetables, protein-rich quinoa, and a zesty dressing, it's a delightful dish that's both satisfying and healthy.

Enjoy this salad as a light lunch or a side dish for your favorite grilled entrees.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Black olives
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions. Once cooked, let it cool.
  2. Chop the cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red bell pepper, red onion, and parsley into small pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and chopped vegetables.
  4. Add crumbled feta cheese and sliced black olives to the bowl.
  5. In a separate small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  6. Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  7. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy!
