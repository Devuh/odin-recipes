Vegetarian Lentil Soup

A hearty and nutritious lentil soup perfect for chilly evenings!

This comforting lentil soup is packed with protein and fiber, making it a satisfying meal on its own. Loaded with vegetables and aromatic spices, every spoonful bursts with flavor.

The recipe is straightforward and requires simple ingredients that you likely already have in your pantry.

Ingredients

  • Green lentils
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable broth
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, saut√© the onions, carrots, and celery in olive oil until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic to the pot and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes with their juices. Stir in the cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot and enjoy!
